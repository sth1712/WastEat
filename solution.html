Help us solve the problem

We aim to have a powerful impact by teaching kids sustainable consumption methods from when they are young. We believe that teaching this from an early age will change habits as they grow and will be resourceful for the planet’s future.

Join us in using different senses to change the mindsets of standards and quality of produce. Some fruits and vegetables may look different and abnormal - but the taste, quality and nutrients are not.

Are you ready to join the movement?

Every school in Norway can sign up and become a part of this sustainable initiative. It does not only benefit farmers who no longer have to throw away perfectly edible produce, but also schools on a tight budget. We are cutting out the middleman and at the same time a lot of unnecessary expenses. Farmers receive fair compensation for their goods and it will still be cheaper than the current practices. Most importantly, we can impact the future generations’ habits to live more sustainably together.

How to get started

Step 1

Sign up for our service! Place your order by informing us how many students will be cooking, and when and where you want the food to be delivered.

Step 2

We collect unused seasonal food items from local farms that have partnered with us. Examples of fruit and vegetables we collect are potatoes, onions, root vegetables, spinach, salads, corn, rhubarb, apples, pears, berries and much more!

Step 3

We assemble and pack in an environmentally friendly cardboard box and attach relevant recipes and teaching resources.

Step 4

We deliver the produce directly to your school by using environmentally friendly transportation methods.

Step 5

Your students use the food and resources in a food and health class, and get to partake in changing the future for the better!
