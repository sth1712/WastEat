How to get started

Step 1

Place your order by selecting the produce you wish to purchase.

Step 2

We collect unused seasonal food items from local farms that have partnered with us. Examples of fruit and vegetables we collect are potatoes, onions, root vegetables, spinach, salads, corn, rhubarb, apples, pears, berries and much more!

Step 3

We assemble and pack in an environmentally friendly cardboard box and attach relevant recipes and teaching resources.

Step 4

We deliver the produce directly to your school by using environmentally friendly transportation methods.

Step 5

Your students use the food and resources in a food and health class, and get to partake in changing the future for the better!
